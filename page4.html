Natlan

Malipo Kinich

A Saurian Hunter from the Scions of the Canopy along with the bearer of the ancient name Malipo.

Mavuika

The Pyro Archon, who relinquished all personal attachments and even her life five hundred years ago to be resurrected in the present day as part of an elaborate, emotionless plan to safeguard Natlan from the Abyss.

Citlali

Citlali, a powerful and ageless shaman of Natlan's Masters of the Night-Wind tribe, lives a secluded life filled with wine, snacks, and light novels, all while grappling with the grief of outliving her mortal friends, especially Huitzilin.

Ororon

Ororon is an eccentric young man of the Masters of the Night-Wind tribe who, after being ostracized for having a "damaged" soul and nearly sacrificed as a child, seeks to find meaning in his life by working with the Fatui Harbinger Capitano to save Natlan.

Mualani

Mualani is a cheerful, well-known guide and surf shop owner from Natlan's People of the Springs tribe who uses her expert knowledge of the land to safely lead travelers, including the Traveler, on their journeys.

Xilonen

Xilonen is a blacksmith in Natlan who is dedicated to her craft of forging, with her lore involving her past as a formidable artisan who forged "Ancient Names" and a potential sacrifice to create a name for the Traveler.
