Fontaine

Neuvillette

Neuvillette is the Hydro Dragon and the Chief Justice of Fontaine, a being of immense power who is the embodiment of the original authority of the world's waters and the successor to the previous Hydro Dragon.

Wriothesley

Wriothesley is the Duke of Fontaine and the administrator of the Fortress of Meropide, a role he assumed after killing his abusive foster parents to save his siblings and being sent to the fortress as punishment, where he later earned respect and took over leadership.

Furina

The human aspect of the Hydro Archon Focalors, was forced to play the role of the infallible deity for 500 years to deceive the Heavenly Principles, ultimately sacrificing her divinity to save the nation of Fontaine and allow its people to become true humans.

Escoffier

Escoffier is a renowned and demanding Fontainian chef, known for her strict standards and ferocious commitment to culinary excellence.

Lyney

Lyney is a renowned magician in Fontaine who, along with his twin sister Lynette, secretly works as an agent for the Fatui's House of the Hearth under the direct command of the Harbinger Arlecchino, navigating a life of performance and espionage while aiming to eventually become "Father's" successor.

Lynette

Lynette is quiet and observant, working as a magician's assistant to her twin brother, Lyney, and serves as an "eye" and "ear" for the Fatui's House of the Hearth organization.

Arlecchino

The current Harbinger of the Fatui who runs the House of the Hearth, formerly known as Crucabena, after she killed the "Mother" figure to protect the children and took on the name "Father" to be a less cruel leader.
