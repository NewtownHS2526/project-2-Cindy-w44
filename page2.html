Liyue

Beidou

Beidou, the formidable Captain of The Crux, is a self-made hero who rose from an impoverished childhood to slay the great sea monster Haishan without a Vision, eventually earning the respect of both her loyal crew and the Liyue Qixing.

Chong yun

Chongyun is a Liyue exorcist with congenital positivity, meaning his extreme yang energy naturally drives away evil spirits, making it difficult for him to find them to perform his duties.

Ganyu

A half-adeptus who has served as the secretary for the Liyue Qixing for over three thousand years, leading to an internal struggle with feeling out of place between the immortal adepti world and the fleeting human one.

Hutao

A eccentric and pragmatic Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor in Liyue, a job she inherited after her grandfather's death, which taught her to embrace life fully by first confronting and understanding death.

Xiao

Xiao is an Anemo Yaksha who, after being freed by Rex Lapis, now battles evil demons to protect Liyue and endures immense suffering from the "karmic debt" of past battles, a burden he carries alone despite his loyalty.

Yunjin

Yun Jin is the director of the Yun-Han Opera Troupe in Liyue, a renowned performer known for her traditional opera skills and for blending it with modern styles to tell stories about ordinary people.
