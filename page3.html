Sumeru

Kaveh

A renowned but indebted architect who carries the guilt of his father's death, which led him to pursue his mother's profession, forcing him to sell his family home and live in debt while maintaining a passionate but often financially ruinous career.

Alhhaithan

Alhaitham is the Scribe of the Sumeru Akademiya, a scholar of the Haravatat Darshan, who prioritizes logic and evidence over emotions, leading many to find him inscrutable despite his goal of living a simple life.

Collei

An experimental subject of the Fatui Harbinger Il Dottore, who injected her with the essence of a dead god to treat a childhood disease, leaving her with both physical and psychological trauma, before she eventually found a new home in Sumeru.

Tighnari

Tighnari is a Forest Watcher in Genshin Impact, a Sumeru native of the Valuka Shuna race who prefers to live in the Avidya Forest where he studies flora and fauna.

Cyno

Cyno is the General Mahamatra of the Sumeru Akademiya, a position that makes him the punisher of those who break the academy's rules, and he channels the divine power of the ancient sage Hermanubis to carry out his duties.

Nahida

Nahida is the Dendro Archon of Sumeru who, after being imprisoned by the Sages, emerged as the God of Wisdom to learn about the world by intervening in people's lives through dreams, and eventually gained knowledge of her predecessor, Greater Lord Rukkhadevata.
