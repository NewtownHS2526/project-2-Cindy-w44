Mondstat

Diluc

Diluc, the owner of the Dawn Winery, was once a knight of the Ordo Favonius, but he left the organization after his father's death during an attack involving a Delusion, leading him to protect Mondstadt in his own way from the shadows.

Kaeya

A spy from Khaenri'ah, left in Mondstadt by his father as the nation's "last hope," but later adopted by the Ragnvindr family and raised alongside Diluc.

Venti

Venti is an ancient wind spirit who became the Anemo Archon, sacrificing his friend to liberate the people of Mondstadt and now hides his true power behind a jovial, often deceptive, persona.

Bennett

An abandoned child who is both cursed by bad luck and blessed to have survived his harsh origins, found in the dangerous Mare Jivari wasteland by adventurers who raised him as part of the Benny's Adventure team.

Klee

Klee is a young, destructive but kind-hearted Spark Knight who was entrusted to the Knights of Favonius by her famous adventurer parents, Alice and her husband, who left her in their care to go on other travels.

Eula

A descendant of the despised Lawrence clan, an aristocratic family of former oppressors, which leads her to seek vengeance on those who wronged her and her family, despite joining the Knights of Favonius to distance herself from their corrupt ideology.
