Tartaglia

Tartaglia is the Eleventh Harbinger of the Fatui who, after getting lost in the Abyss as a youth, was trained by an immortal swordswoman, leading to his love for battle and his complex, multifaceted personality.

Flins

Flins is an ancient Snowland Fae noble who, after witnessing the end of the Belyi Tsar's era, went into a centuries-long slumber before being awakened by the human Lightkeepers to help them fight the Abyssal Wild Hunt in the region of Nod-Krai, where he now serves as a lone guardian of a lighthouse and cemetery.
