Inazuma

Featuring the top characters of this nation!

Yae Miko

Yae Miko is the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine and a cunning kitsune who is a long-time friend and confidante of the Electro Archon, Ei, and also runs the Yae Publishing House.

Raiden Shogun

Raiden Ei is the current Electro Archon of Inazuma, who, after her twin sister Makoto's death, created a puppet to rule as the Raiden Shogun while she meditates in seclusion to pursue the ideal of eternity for her nation.

Gorou

Gorou is a courageous and loyal, dog-eared general who rose through the ranks of the Watatsumi Army to lead the resistance forces during the Vision Hunt Decree, earning the deep respect of his troops for his humble nature and ability to inspire unity in battle.

Sangonomiya Kokomi

Sangonomiya Kokomi is the young, introverted Divine Priestess and a brilliant military strategist who reluctantly leads Watatsumi Island's resistance movement against the Raiden Shogun's Vision Hunt Decree while managing the immense pressure of her people's expectations.

Thoma

Thoma is the ever-loyal, sociable, and resourceful "fixer" for the Kamisato Clan in Inazuma, who originally came from Mondstadt and uses his extensive network of connections to resolve any issue, especially those threatening the clan's interests.

Wanderer

Scaramouche, originally created as an emotionaless puppet to hold the Electro Gnosis, was discarded by his creator, Raiden Ei, and after enduring a series of devastating betrayals and loss at the hands of humans, he developed a profound hatred for the world and gods, which led him to become a Fatui Harbinger and eventually attempt to become a god himself before having his past mostly erased from Irminsul.
